# License change: MIT → CC BY-SA 4.0

## What landed

- Replaced MIT license with CC BY-SA 4.0 in `LICENSE` and `README.md`.
- Merged via PR #3.

## Decisions

- **CC BY-SA 4.0 over other CC variants.** BY-SA keeps the content
  free for commercial use while requiring attribution and share-alike
  — fits an open skill collection where derivatives should stay open.
  BY-NC was considered but would block commercial agent platforms from
  bundling skills. Plain BY (no SA) was considered but doesn't ensure
  derivative collections remain open.
- **Human-readable summary in LICENSE, not the full legal code.** The
  full legal text is 4,000+ words. The LICENSE file links to the
  canonical legalcode URL; the summary is the standard CC deed text.

## Deferred

- Nothing.
